Considering Levemir®? Choose Tresiba®
As you know, there is another U-100 option from Novo Nordisk with added features. Starting patients on Tresiba® is similar to starting patients on Levemir®. Both options have the same concentration, a similar starting dose, and are administered using the FlexTouch® device.(2,3,a) However, clinical data show that Tresiba® has even more to offer. And, out-of-pocket costs may be lower for Tresiba®.
(a)Tresiba® is also available in a U-200 concentration.
